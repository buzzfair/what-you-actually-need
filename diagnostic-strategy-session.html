The 60-minute structure

What we will do

15

First 15 minutes

Surface the real bottleneck. Not the presenting symptom — the underlying constraint that is creating drag across your business.

20

Next 20 minutes

Audit the current approach. What you have tried, what has partially worked, what the data or pattern is actually saying.

15

Next 15 minutes

Narrow options. Given your situation, capacity, and goals, which moves are worth making — and which are noise.

10

Final 10 minutes

Set the next step. One clear, specific action. Not a list. Not a framework. A next move.
